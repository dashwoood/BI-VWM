Northern Ireland Secretary Karen Bradley will make a statement in the House of Commons on Tuesday in relation to the murder.

Mrs Bradley will be among political leaders who will attend Ms McKee's funeral on Wednesday.

Irish Foreign Minister Simon Coveney is in Northern Ireland meeting some of the Stormont parties after calls on Monday for urgent talks to be convened to restore power-sharing.

Protecting witnesses
Ms McKee was standing near a police 4x4 vehicle when she was shot after a masked gunman fired towards police and onlookers.

A protest by friends of Ms McKee took place on Monday outside the office of Saoradh.

A number of women smeared red paint in handprints on republican slogans outside the office.

Police were present but did not make any immediate arrests.
